Prevention Practices for Water Pollution

Sources of Water Pollution

Water pollution can come from many sources, including:

  • Industrial waste
  • Agricultural runoff
  • Sewage and wastewater
  • Oil and chemical spills
  • Litter and garbage

Prevention Techniques

There are several techniques that individuals and communities can use to prevent water pollution:

  • Reduce the use of fertilizers and pesticides
  • Properly dispose of hazardous waste
  • Plant trees and other vegetation near waterways to absorb pollutants
  • Use biodegradable soaps and detergents
  • Reduce water usage

Take Action

It is important for everyone to take action to prevent water pollution. Here are some steps you can take:

  • Dispose of hazardous waste properly
  • Reduce water usage
  • Participate in community cleanup events
  • Advocate for policies that protect water quality
